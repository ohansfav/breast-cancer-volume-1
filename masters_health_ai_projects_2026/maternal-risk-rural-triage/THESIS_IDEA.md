# Thesis Idea: Maternal Risk Rural Triage

## Working Title
Risk-Calibrated Maternal Referral Triage for Rural Health Systems Using Interpretable Machine Learning

## Problem
Rural maternal care often faces delayed referral, limited transport, and scarce specialists. A triage score can help prioritize the highest-risk pregnancies.

## Hypothesis
A threshold-aware, interpretable classifier can identify high-risk maternal cases with enough sensitivity to improve referral prioritization.

## Methods
- Simulate maternal risk with care-access features
- Train a baseline classifier
- Evaluate discrimination, calibration, and referral tradeoffs
- Discuss fairness and access barriers

## Contribution
The thesis can frame maternal triage as a decision-support problem under resource scarcity, not just a classification benchmark.
