# Thesis Idea: Hospital Bed Demand Forecast

## Working Title
Short-Horizon Hospital Bed Demand Forecasting for Surge Preparedness Using Interpretable Machine Learning

## Problem
Hospitals need better short-term bed forecasts to plan staffing, admissions, and escalation during outbreaks or seasonal surges.

## Hypothesis
A simple, interpretable regression model can forecast bed pressure well enough to support operational planning and surge alerts.

## Methods
- Simulate bed pressure with seasonality and community transmission features
- Train a regression model
- Evaluate MAE, RMSE, and R2
- Translate forecast output into watch/surge policy bands

## Contribution
The thesis can show that interpretable machine learning is enough for many planning tasks and can be extended later with real hospital data.
